The Celebration Details

When is the Event?

September 12, 2025, at 6:00 PM. (Download to my calendar | Add to google calendar)

Where is the Event?

Space Gallery in Denver, CO.

Will there be parking?

There will be some parking at and near the venue. You can find more information on parking here.

Is there a Dress Code?

We encourage people to dress in whatever makes them comfortable and look forward to seeing what that looks like. However, for those looking for some dress-peration, we suggest summer cocktail attire.

What’s the Weather like in Denver at that time of year?

The weather in Denver in September can be unpredictable, but typically we see highs in the 70’s and lows in the 50s or 60s. While the venue will have both indoor and outdoor space, please come prepared for a big swing in temperature after the sun goes down.

Are kids invited?

Kids of all ages and sizes are more than welcome to attend, but the amount of kid-friendly activities provided may be limited. Please let us know if you plan to bring your kids and we can increase the amount of steak knives and fireworks on site.

Should we bring a gift?

Unfortunately, we are unable to accept any gifts of any kind, due to the fact that we don’t want to. If, however, you wish to contribute to our future together, you still cannot give us any gifts.

What will we be doing at the Reception?

While we won’t have many of the traditional wedding activities (like an actual ceremony where we get married, which is typical), you can expect plenty of food, dancing, socialization, and more dancing.

Will there be food?

Yes! There will be food during cocktail hour as well as a taco buffet for dinner. If you have any dietary restrictions, please let us know.

Will there be alcohol?

Obviously.

Will transportation be provided?

While we aren’t providing transportation to/from the venue, there will be plenty of parking and Uber/Lyft available. Alex has also offered up his top of the line road, gravel, and mountain bikes to anyone looking for an alternative form of transportation - first come, first serve.

Is there a Theme?

While 50% of us would like there to be a theme, there will not be a theme. At least not a theme that 50% of us know about.

If we are travelling to Denver, where should we stay?

Our "Super sophisticated and unbiased map" is being updated as we think of things. It has some neighborhood descriptions and hotel options. The recently constructed Sonder Denver Skyline is a good option (close to our house) if you are a Marriott loyalist who wants an apartment style hotel.
